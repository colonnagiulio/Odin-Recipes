Lasagna!

Why Lasagna?

Lasagna is very skill-oriented and very time consuming recipe to pull off! When executed correctly however, everyone will be rushing back to the kitchen for seconds! In this begginner friendly recipe I will give you the 6 steps to complete in order to be able to enjoy this fantastic delicacy.

Lasagna dates all the way back to the 14th century, from the rich culinary region of Naples, Italy. Lasagna was brought to the Romans when they conquered areas of Greece where the dish was originally introduced to them. The Romans embraced other cultures and the next thing you know it became an Italian household dish for special occasions!

What Ingredients Do I Need?

  • Meat:You're going to need Italian sausage mixed in with mostly ground beef
  • Onions and Garlic:A whole Onion and 2 cloves of Garlic are required to give our meat some flavour!
  • Fresh Tomatoes:Essentiel for creating our sauce for our Lasagna!
  • Sugar:You don't need much only two tablespoons to give the sauce some sweetness
  • Spices and Seasonning:This lasagna recipe is flavored with fresh parsley, dried basil leaves, salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and black pepper.
  • Lasagna Pasta Sheets:You could eighter use home made or sorebought the descision is up to you
  • Cheeses:Parmesan, mozzarella, and ricotta cheese make this lasagna extra decadent.
  • Egg:An egg helps bind the ricotta so it doesn't ooze out of the lasagna when you cut into it.

How To Make Lasagna Step By Step

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.

How To Layer Your Lasagna

  1. Meat sauce
  2. Noodles
  3. Ricotta mixture
  4. Mozzarella slices
  5. Meat sauce
  6. Parmesan cheese
  7. Repeat the layers, then top with the remaining Parmesan.
